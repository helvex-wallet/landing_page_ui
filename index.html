Why RFQ on Canton

Public order books leak. Private quotes don't.

Every swap on a public chain broadcasts your intent before it executes. On Canton, negotiation happens between you and your makers. Nobody else sees it.

01 · pricing

Firm, not indicative

Every quote is executable at the shown price for its full lifetime. The price you accept is the price you settle. Slippage isn't reduced. It's structurally absent.

02 · privacy

No mempool, no MEV

Canton has no public mempool to snipe. Your request is visible only to the makers you query. Front-running needs an audience, and Helvex doesn't give it one.

03 · settlement

Atomic, both legs

Acceptance and settlement execute as one Canton transaction. Either both sides of the swap complete, or nothing moves. No counterparty leg risk, no escrow limbo.

How it works

Four steps. One transaction.
